Liverpool Victoria
LV= is one of the UK's leading insurance companies. They are also active investors in corporate wellness and value the wellbeing of their employees. The company is actively exploring the use of wearables and quantified self data for improving the way they deliver corporate wellness campaigns. "Nuwe is working with LV= on a corporate wellness solution designed to promote healthy lifestyle choices amongst their employees. The project includes the creation of a leaderboard app that will be delivered via the Mendix driven intranet system. Corporate Wellness
Steve Schofield
CTO & Co-Founder
Corporate Wellness
"Many companies do not implement a meaningful wellness strategy for a simple reason: with no real measure of where the organisation should focus its resources, it can be difficult to know where to start. They often struggle to answer key questions: is our productivity increasing? Are our employees healthier? Is the return on investment acceptable?" "Nuwe helps you control your metrics and gives a meaningful picture of your wellness programmes. It shows where you need to direct funds, and whether what you’re doing is actually delivering results. Hopi allows company employees to create accounts, make teams and connect their wearables of choice to privately track their daily health data. The data is turned into a Nuscore which is sent in real-time from the Nuwe platform to the Hopi Box. The Hopi Box connects this information to a large format device, like a TV, to display the company health dashboard."
Meschino Wellness
Steve Schofield
CTO & Co-Founder
Meschino Wellness
Meschino Wellness is an employee's wellness platform. Our brief was to rethink the interface application and user experience for the Health Risk Assessment (HRA) engine, around which the platform functions. We were asked to simplify the process and make the service more consumer friendly. "Nuwe conducted a series of focus groups to test the HRA platform and identify the areas that needed improvement. Based on the results of these tests we restructured the engine, and rewrote the questions to improve speed of comprehension and the unaided completion of the HRA. We also ensured the engine was dynamic, displaying relevant questions based on the answers users had provided. Nuwe then undertook a complete overhaul of the user interface. This process included improving the on-boarding messages and creating a design that both guided users as well as rewarding them for participation."
Nutribu for iOS
Steve Schofield
CTO & Co-Founder
Nutribu for iOS
Comparing calorie intake between people who are trying to lose weight involves significant difficulties. Since every individual's goals for nutrients differ, there is no reliable way of sharing knowledge to achieve goals. "Nutribu is Nuwe’s nutrition app. It answers a clear need to make food tracking simpler and more engaging. With Nutribu installed on your smartphone, everything you eat is combined into a single number - your NuScore. Nutribu then gently and positively provides you with relevant real-time nutritional advice. Learning from your eating habits, Nutribu makes suggestions to help you achieve health and wellness goals. The content and mechanic combine to provide a simple and useful experience."
NuweApp: Value of Wellness
Steve Schofield
CTO & Co-Founder
NuweApp: Value of Wellness
Digital wellness solutions today are fragmented and isolated. They fail to communicate the value of wellness. So we re-imagined wellness as a universally understood unit of currency powered by actionable information, derived from connected data. "Nuwe has created a common language which allows apps and devices to speak to each other. This means that people, teams and organisations can build a true picture of wellness. The Nuwe Value of Wellness - your Nuscore - is a personal measure that helps people understand their behaviour and make informed decisions to reach wellness goals. Nuscore is designed to remove the fragmentation and isolation of wellness data. It is made up of multiple data points that are collected, analysed and shared by apps built on the Nuwe platform. Nuwe creates a single ecosystem in which wellness data, Internet of Things technologies and health professionals’ discoveries combine."
