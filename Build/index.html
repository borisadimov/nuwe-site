Making Technology Personal
A message from Sergio Mottola
We believe every user has the power to be a producer and a maker, not just a consumer. It’s our mission to transform the status of users. We think innovation must serve the greater good, and that it should pioneer systems to make our world a better place populated by happier people. What makes people happy is creating, not just consuming. Too much consumer-based technology keeps us ‘below' digital services, and it creates fragmentation and incoherence; anxiety and inefficiency. We prefer to build technology that elevates the user above the digital services they use. We are all happier when we're making things. To achieve this we are enabling functionally integrated, personal IoT services that use innovative technology solutions to enhance life experience. Nuwe believes that when digital solutions serve personal matters, such as health and wellness, or growth and performance, those services require a high degree of personalisation, or as it's more commonly termed, 'customisation'. It is difficult to imagine that one type of service or application, designed by a small team, could be consumed globally. So we decided to create a new world in which the role of technology is to provide a multitude of components, empowering us all to build digital services.

NuweApp: Value of Wellness
Steve Schofield
CTO & Co-Founder
NuweApp: Value of Wellness
Digital wellness solutions today are fragmented and isolated. They fail to communicate the value of wellness. So we re-imagined wellness as a universally understood unit of currency powered by actionable information, derived from connected data. "Nuwe has created a common language which allows apps and devices to speak to each other. This means that people, teams and organisations can build a true picture of wellness. The Nuwe Value of Wellness - your Nuscore - is a personal measure that helps people understand their behaviour and make informed decisions to reach wellness goals. Nuscore is designed to remove the fragmentation and isolation of wellness data. It is made up of multiple data points that are collected, analysed and shared by apps built on the Nuwe platform. Nuwe creates a single ecosystem in which wellness data, Internet of Things technologies and health professionals’ discoveries combine."

Corporate Wellness
Steve Schofield
CTO & Co-Founder
Corporate Wellness
"Many companies do not implement a meaningful wellness strategy for a simple reason: with no real measure of where the organisation should focus its resources, it can be difficult to know where to start. They often struggle to answer key questions: is our productivity increasing? Are our employees healthier? Is the return on investment acceptable?" "Nuwe helps you control your metrics and gives a meaningful picture of your wellness programmes. It shows where you need to direct funds, and whether what you’re doing is actually delivering results. Hopi allows company employees to create accounts, make teams and connect their wearables of choice to privately track their daily health data. The data is turned into a Nuscore which is sent in real-time from the Nuwe platform to the Hopi Box. The Hopi Box connects this information to a large format device, like a TV, to display the company health dashboard."
